Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

How do I book a Ride Vista Cab?

You can easily book a Ride Vista Cab through our website. Simply fill up the 'Book my ride' form and confirm your booking. We will contact you within 15 minutes.

What safety measures are in place during the ride?

At Ride Vista Cabs, your safety is our top priority. All our cabs are regularly sanitized, and our drivers follow strict hygiene protocols. We also offer contactless payment options for a seamless and safe experience.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pick-up time without any cancellation charges. If you cancel within 2 hours of the pick-up time, a cancellation fee will apply.

Can I modify my booking after it's been confirmed?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pick-up time.
